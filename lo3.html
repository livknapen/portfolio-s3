Clear starting points

For starting with my iterations, I created a clear starting point to build on. This way I could keep track on how I made iterations. I always start on the left side and work towards right for small iterations. When I make a whole new design, I work downwards. I get this tip form a teachers last year and I think it is a good way to keep track of your iterations.
