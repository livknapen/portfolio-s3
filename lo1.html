Target audience

By creating a good product for our stakeholder, we have defined a target audience for each project. This gave us a good idea of ​​what appeals to our audience. This also allowed us to conduct good surveys to get in touch with this audience.


Concept

For every project I made a concept document to know what the goals are for the project and what we as a group going to make for our stakeholder. This also allows me to document every detail of what my projects are about. I think it is a good way to know what the whole projects are aboutt.


Design

For my projects, I made different designs. From websites to logo's to posters. All designs started with doing research and inspiration, and with the feedback I received on different products, I could improve the designs. With doing this proces, I could make good designs along with strong arguments as to why I made something this way.


Proof of concept

To get a good idea of what the Voting Guide website would look like with the statements you can vote on, I first made a Concept. This allows me to see how it show the data and how it looks, and with this information I can ultimately process the real statements.


Interactive product

For our project we made an interactive product for the website. With making this interactive, we can test this design with our target audience. And with this feedback we can make our design better. Also, for making it interactive so we know what to link to the pages.
