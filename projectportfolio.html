PORTFOLIO

The concept for my portfolio is of course to demonstrate my learning goals for this semester. Also to be able to show every project.

First you come to the homepage where there is a photo of me and my name, this is immediately personal and as a user you know who you are reading it from. If you scroll down, you will see all the projects I contributed to this semester. You can click per project to see a summary of the entire project.

In the learning outcomes you can see what I have done for each learning outcome. There will be a PDF for each learning outcome where everything is described in detail.

Finally, there is a page where I write a piece about me.
